2019 ALWFAV3 Paloma Moniz, Ricardo Gonçalves, Jaime Del Corro, Margarida Morais, Francisca Roseiro - Publication, Symposium

A Line Which Forms A Volume 3 is a critical reader of graphic design-led research that is edited, written, designed and published by participants of the MA Graphic Media Design course at London College of Communication (LCC). The new issue focuses on collaboration and interdependence, metaphorically using the idea of the roundabout, once a landmark of Elephant and Castle (where LCC is located at), as a backbone that guides editorial and visual dynamics in unexpected ways. The reader is structured in entries as chapters, sporadically interrupted by intersections, filled with exits and centred by a 12-black-page collective contribution embodying the monument. Alongside visual essays, poems and critical writings from MA GMD graduates, the issue features contributions from guests Evening Class, Anja Kaiser and Rebecca Stephany, Legrand Jäger, and Paul Soulellis.
