In the course of the Reciprocal Studio, a workshop series in collaboration with guest tutors, our hub was confronted with the subject of distraction and the optimisation in todays society. Premium Hustler shows a critical position, responding to the scarcity of idle time within a society driven by the notion of productivity. By identifying the polar sense of idleness and productivity, the value of the mere image of productivity is, thus, the logical conclusion. The significance of this active performance is represented by the massive emergence of online coaches. By combining the concept of the coach and the reconstructed image of productivity, we developed the philosophy, objects and platforms for Premium Hustler. The persona functions to democratise idleness but at the same time, parodies and plays with the inverted image of productivity and the expectations of the viewer. While the appearance is inspired by our enquiry into productivity coaches, the developed visual language accomplishes a dissonance. The visual language follows the approach of camouflage and distraction, translated into transparency and blurriness. Distraction as an additional method arises within elements like typeface, pattern and 3D objects. The use of the colour grey represents the wasteland of office landscapes, the dominant green parodies the coach.
