Roxy Zeiher is a graphic designer living and working between Berlin and London. Particularly interested in critical and contemporary subject areas communicated through design, she pursues a multi-disciplinary creative practice. She interrogates culture with her research-driven design practice that investigates how the designed world, especially new technology, is shaping our behaviour, perception and understanding. She is 1/4 of TROJAN HORSE Club, a creative collective aiming to infiltrate contexts and subjects of cultural and social relevance by using the veneer and shell of design.
